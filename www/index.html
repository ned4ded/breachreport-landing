What is BreachReport?

BreachReport is a digital security platform with the largest database of publicly displayed user information, created from over 10,000 separate data breaches, holding information on over 9 billion compromised emails that is constantly updated.

BreachReport focuses on providing solutions for safe internet use, promoting cybersecurity awareness and bringing exclusive breach investigations into the spotlight.
