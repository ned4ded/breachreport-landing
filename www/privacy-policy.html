Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2019

Breach Report (pending registration) provides a service that enables users to check whether their personal emails (or other emails issued and managed under a domain name owned by them (“Domain Owners”)) were leaked to the internet following a data breach (the “Service”).

We take your privacy very seriously and this is why we are providing you with this Privacy Policy, which clearly states how we collect information about you (whether you are a private user or a Domain Owner), what we do with the information we collect, how we share it with third parties and how you control your information. This Privacy Policy also describes the security procedures in place to protect the misuse of your personal information.

This privacy policy applies solely to information collected by this web site. Breach Report is the controller and is responsible for your personal information (“Company”, “we”, “us” or “our”). Breach Report is currently pending registration.

The term “personal information” as used in this privacy policy shall mean any information that enables us to identify you, directly or indirectly, by reference to an identifier such as your name.

We may change our Privacy Policy at our discretion from time to time upon providing you notice of such changes, as appropriate, and updating the “Last updated” date at the top of the Privacy Policy. Your continued use of our Services confirms your acceptance of our updated Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to our updated Privacy Policy, you must stop using the Services.

What information do we collect?

We use different methods to collect personal information from and about you including:

Personal information you give us. You may give us your personal information including name and contact details, by filling in forms or by corresponding with us by post, phone, email or otherwise. This includes personal information you provide when you ask us about, or apply for, our products or services; subscribe to our services or publications; request marketing to be sent to you or give us some feedback. Although some of the features of our Service may not require any registration, in order to use the full scope of this web site, a user must first sign up (we might decide to require registration to other parts of the Service in the future). During registration a user is required to give certain information such as your email, name, etc.

Some information is being collected automatically. For example, we collect diagnostic, performance and maintenance information about your activity, your use of the Services, log files and other reports. We also collect device-specific information when you use the Services. We use third-party analytics software to analyze user behavior and improve our Services.

This may include information such as your mobile device type, operating system information, browser information, IP addresses and network information including device identifiers. If you use location features, we may collect device location information.

If you have paid for any of our Services, we may receive payment information such as credit cards details, billing address etc., as well as other information and confirmations, such as payment receipts, including from app stores or other third parties (including payment solutions providers) processing your payment.

Cookies

We use cookies to operate, improve and customize the Services and to improve your experience. A cookie is a small text file that a website you visit asks your browser to store on your computer or mobile device.

We may also use cookies to show you relevant content related to the Services. Finally, we may use cookies to remember your preferences, such as language and other choices you made using our Services. You can follow the instructions on your browser or device in order to control your cookie settings. Please note that if you set your browser or device to disable cookies (in whole or in part), certain of our Services may not function properly or at all.

We also use your information to communicate with you regarding the Services, respond to you when you contact us, let you know about updates to our Services or to our Terms and other important updates.

Please note that we may also use your information if we believe that it is reasonably required to be compliant with any applicable laws or regulations, court orders or other legal proceedings, requests from governmental entities, to enforce our Terms and polices, to investigate and prevent fraud or other illegal activity or for other security or technical issues including without limitations to protect you and our other users.

Unless you ask us not to, we may contact you via email in the future to tell you about specials, new products or services, or changes to this privacy policy. We will ask for your consent to receive marketing communications in line with the applicable law when you first provide your personal information.

In case we will allow any third-party ads on the Services or any other commercial content, we will make sure this is done in compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

Sharing your personal information

We share your information mainly as reasonably necessary in order to provide you with our Services, to improve them, to customize them to your specific needs, to perform support and maintenance to our Services, to develop new services and features and to market our Services so you and your connections can have a more enjoyable and rewarding experience.

How we store your data

Your personal data and its security is taken very seriously by us and so we use all reasonable efforts to ensure appropriate security measures to protect it. However, there is no way to guarantee 100% protection to information which is being transmitted via the internet.. Please note however, that we will follow strict procedures to protect your data and prevent unauthorized access.

The data we collect from you is stored in a fully PCI-DSS compliant data center located in Europe, which is protected by the strictest securities policies and measures.

How long do we keep your personal information?

We will store your personal information, in a form which permits us to identify you, for no longer than is necessary for the purpose for which the personal information is processed. We use your personal information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements and rights, or if it is not technically and reasonably feasible to remove it. Otherwise, we will seek to delete your personal information within a reasonable timeframe upon request.

Your rights with respect to your information

We would like you to have full control over your information and to be able to manage it.

You may contact us at any time and request: (i) that your information will be deleted (including to delete your account); (ii) to change any information we collected about you; (iii) to know what information we have collected about you; (iv) any other request or inquiry regarding your information, your privacy or your rights as you are entitled under applicable law from time to time; (v) express a concern you have about our use of your personal information; (vi) have us transmit any personal information directly to another organization, in certain circumstances; or (vii) lodge a complaint with a competent data protection authority.

Please direct all such inquiries to: dpo@breachreport.com

Security Measures

We take precautions to protect your information. When you submit sensitive information via the web site, your information is protected both online and offline. Wherever we collect sensitive information, the data is encrypted and transmitted to us in a secure way and we keep it hashed with one-way encryption algorithms.

While we use encryption to protect sensitive information transmitted online, we also protect your information offline. Only employees who need the information to perform a specific job (for example, billing or customer service) are granted access to personally identifiable information. The computers/servers in which we store personally identifiable information are kept in a secure environment.

The Services may contain links to other websites maintained by third parties. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our web site and to read the privacy statements of any other site that collects personal information.
